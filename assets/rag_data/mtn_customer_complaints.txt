MTN NIGERIA - COMMON CUSTOMER COMPLAINTS & RESOLUTIONS

=== NETWORK & CONNECTIVITY ===

Complaint: Poor network signal / dropped calls in my area
Category: Network Quality
Common Causes: Distance from cell tower, physical obstructions, network congestion during peak hours
Resolution Steps:
1. Check MTN's network coverage map on the website or myMTN app
2. Restart your phone to force a fresh network registration
3. Toggle airplane mode on and off
4. Report the location specifically to MTN via call 300, WhatsApp (09033000001), or the myMTN app so it can be logged for engineering review
5. For persistent issues, escalate to the NCC (080-22220002) if unresolved after 30 days

Complaint: Slow internet / 4G showing but speed is very poor
Category: Data Quality
Common Causes: Network congestion, wrong APN settings, background apps consuming bandwidth
Resolution Steps:
1. Check APN settings: APN should be "internet" for MTN Nigeria
2. Restart mobile data (toggle off and on)
3. Check if data balance is exhausted — dial *323#
4. Test at a different time of day (peak hours 7pm–10pm often have congestion)
5. Report via 300 or myMTN app with your location

=== DATA & BILLING COMPLAINTS ===

Complaint: My data is finishing too quickly / data is being deducted without use
Category: Data Consumption
Common Causes: Background app refresh, auto-download, subscribed VAS services
Resolution Steps:
1. Dial *323# to check all active balances and identify which bucket is being consumed
2. Check for active subscriptions draining airtime: Dial *312*5# to see and cancel subscriptions
3. Turn off background data for non-essential apps
4. Disable WhatsApp auto-download: Settings → Storage and data → Media auto-download
5. If the deduction is unexplained, call 300 and request a data usage log

Complaint: I was charged for a service I didn't subscribe to
Category: Unauthorized Charge / VAS Subscription
Resolution Steps:
1. Dial *312*5# to view all active subscriptions
2. Cancel any unwanted subscription immediately from the same menu
3. Call 300 or email customercare.ng@mtn.com to request a refund and provide: your number, the service name, amount deducted, and date
4. If unresolved within 14 days, escalate to NCC consumer portal at ncc.gov.ng

Complaint: Failed airtime or data transaction — money deducted but service not received
Category: Failed Transaction
Resolution Steps:
1. Wait 10–15 minutes as delayed processing sometimes resolves it
2. Check your balance via *310# (airtime) or *323# (data)
3. If still unresolved: dial 300, chat with Zigi on WhatsApp at 09033000001, or email customercare.ng@mtn.com
4. Provide your transaction reference if you have it
5. Track your complaint via the "Track Complaint" feature on myMTN app using your Ticket Ref ID

Complaint: My data bundle expired before I could use it all
Category: Bundle Expiry
Resolution Steps:
1. Unused data can roll over — but only if you renew before expiry and on an equal or larger plan
2. Always renew before expiry, not after
3. Check expiry date by dialling *131*1#
4. Consider switching to a monthly plan if daily/weekly plans expire too fast

=== CUSTOMER SERVICE COMPLAINTS ===

Complaint: Long wait times when calling 300 / unable to reach an agent
Category: Customer Service Quality
Resolution Steps:
1. Try WhatsApp at 09033000001 — often faster than calling
2. Use live chat on mtn.ng during off-peak hours (early morning)
3. Use the myMTN app self-service features for common issues (balance, data, subscriptions)
4. Visit a physical MTN service centre for complex issues
Note: You will first interact with Zigi (MTN's AI chatbot). If your issue is not resolved, you will be transferred to a live agent.

Complaint: Rude or unhelpful customer service agent
Category: Agent Conduct
Resolution Steps:
1. Note the time of the call and the agent's name/ID if given
2. Email customercare.ng@mtn.com with a formal complaint including the interaction details
3. Mention that all calls are recorded for quality assurance and request a review
4. Escalate to the FCCPC (fccpc.gov.ng) if MTN does not respond within 14 days

=== ROAMING COMPLAINTS ===

Complaint: My line is not working abroad / unable to make calls while roaming
Category: International Roaming
Resolution Steps:
1. Ensure roaming is activated before travelling — dial *312*6# or visit a service centre
2. Check if your destination is in MTN's roaming partner list on mtn.ng
3. Manually select the network: Go to phone Settings → Mobile Network → Select Network Manually
4. Use the myMTN app to manage roaming if USSD codes are not working abroad
5. Call +2348031000180 for support from outside Nigeria

=== SIM COMPLAINTS ===

Complaint: SIM card not working / unable to make calls or use data
Category: SIM Issue
Resolution Steps:
1. Remove and reinsert the SIM card
2. Test the SIM in another phone to determine if it's a device or SIM issue
3. Restart phone
4. If still not working, visit any MTN service centre for a SIM replacement — bring a valid ID

Complaint: My line has been barred / suspended
Category: Line Suspension
Common Causes: Unpaid postpaid bill, NIN/SIM registration non-compliance, suspected fraud
Resolution Steps:
1. Check if your NIN is properly linked — visit an MTN service centre
2. For postpaid: settle outstanding bills via the myMTN app or at a service centre
3. Call 300 from another line or visit a service centre for clarification
