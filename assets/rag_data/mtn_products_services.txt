MTN NIGERIA - PRODUCTS AND SERVICES OVERVIEW

=== VOICE SERVICES ===

MTN Prepaid
- Pay-as-you-go service with no monthly commitment
- Multiple tariff plans available (Pulse, BetaTalk, and others)
- Check current plan: *123*1*2#
- Migrate between plans via USSD

MTN Postpaid
- Monthly billing with higher-tier service options
- Suitable for business users and heavy callers
- Includes itemised billing and dedicated account management

MTN TravelPass (International Roaming)
- Available to all prepaid and postpaid customers
- Allows calls, SMS, and data while abroad
- Activate via *312*6*1# or *123*4*1# before travelling
- Support from abroad: +2348031000180

=== DATA SERVICES ===

Mobile Data Plans
- Daily Plans: Short-validity bundles for light, occasional use
- Weekly Plans: 7-day bundles for moderate users
- Monthly Plans: Best value for regular internet users
- Bi-monthly / Quarterly / Yearly: Long-term plans with better per-MB rates
- Night Plans: Data valid only between 11pm and 6am — good value for heavy downloaders
- Social Bundles: Restricted to specific apps (WhatsApp, Instagram, Twitter/X)
- Activate all plans: *312# or *904#

5G Network
- MTN operates Nigeria's number one 5G network
- Available in select cities and expanding
- Compatible devices required

MTN Router/Home Broadband Plans
- Fixed broadband data for routers and MiFi devices
- Activate via *312*500# or *461*1#

=== DIGITAL & VALUE-ADDED SERVICES ===

myMTN App
- Self-service app for balance checks, data purchase, bill payment, and complaint tracking
- Download from Play Store or Apple Store, or text "myMTN" to 5018
- Provides bonus data for regular app usage

Zigi (AI Chatbot)
- MTN's virtual assistant available on WhatsApp (09033000001) and the myMTN app
- Handles: balance checks, data subscriptions, complaint logging, FAQ
- Escalates to live agent for complex issues
- Available 24/7

XtraTime (Airtime Credit)
- Borrow airtime or data when balance is low
- Dial *303# to access
- 15% service charge applies
- Repaid automatically on next recharge

MTN Mobile Money (MoMo)
- Mobile financial services platform
- Send and receive money, pay bills, buy data/airtime
- Available via *671# or the MoMo app

=== BUSINESS SERVICES ===

MTN Business Solutions
- Enterprise connectivity (dedicated internet, MPLS, SD-WAN)
- Bulk SMS and voice solutions for businesses
- IoT connectivity for smart devices and fleet management
- Cloud and hosting services

MTN Managed Services
- End-to-end network management for enterprises
- SLA-backed uptime guarantees
